About ELDERLY

ELDERLY is a voice-first AI companion designed for dignified aging. It provides calm conversations, personalized storytelling, memory exercises, and family engagement features to help elders stay mentally active, socially connected, and reassured.

Our mission is to combine AI technology with empathy, creating a safe, supportive, and enjoyable experience for elders worldwide. With ELDERLY, families can ensure their loved ones are cared for in a meaningful, interactive way.
